First Time User Registration for Interlibrary Loan

Libraries using our ILLiad for the first time should contact the ILL office to setup an account by e-mail ils@fairfield.edu and provide us with this information

  • Library name
  • Library address
  • OCLC Symbol (if known)
  • Other Symbol
  • Contact person
  • Dept email
  • CCar # (if applicable)

Once your account has been created, you will have secure access to information about your requests. You will also be able to check the status of your requests and review the list of items you have requested in the past.
